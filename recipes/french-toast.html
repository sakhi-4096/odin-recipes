French Toast Recipe

This is the best French toast recipe. It's different becuase it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make. Enjoy!

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs, sugar, and typically milk, then pan fired. Alternative names and variants include "eggy bread", "Bombay toast", "gypsy toast" and "poor knights"

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Measure four into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or fying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot
