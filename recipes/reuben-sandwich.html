Reuben Sandwich Recipe

These sandwiches are really delicious and easy to make. They are one of my family's fix-t quick favorites. I like to serve them with big bowls of steaming vegetable soup and dill pickles on the side. Enjoy!

The Reuben sandwich is a North Amei=rican grilled sandwich composed of corned beed, Swiss cheese, sauserkraut, and Thousand Island dressing or Russian dressing, grilled between slices of rye bread. It is associated with kosher-style delicatessness, but is not kosher becauseit combines meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup sauserkaraut, drained
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet or griddle om medium heat.
  2. Lightly butter one side of bread slices. Sread non-buttered sides with Thousand Island dressing. On 4 bread slices, layer 1 slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cyo saucerkraut and second slice of Swiss cheese. Top with remaining bread slices, butteres sides out.
  3. Grill sandwiches until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side, Serve hot
